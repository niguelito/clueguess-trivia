# **App Name**: ClueGuess Trivia

## Core Features:

- Dynamic Clue Card Display: Renders a trivia card showing the primary clue, a set of additional progressive clues (initially hidden), and the final answer, all visible to the app operator.
- Progressive Clue Reveal Control: Allows the app operator to manually reveal additional clues one by one, giving them control over the game's difficulty and pace.
- Answer Visibility Toggle: Enables the app operator to display or hide the correct answer, facilitating the two-player game flow.
- AI Trivia Card Generation: Utilizes an AI tool to dynamically generate unique trivia questions, complete with a main clue, multiple progressive sub-clues, and the accurate answer, based on selected categories.
- Trivia Category Selection: Provides a selection interface for the app operator to choose specific trivia categories (e.g., History, Science, Pop Culture) that will influence the generated cards.
- Next Card Functionality: A clear control for the app operator to generate and display a new trivia card instantly, starting a new round.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated, slightly desaturated blue (#4D4DB8) to evoke intelligence, focus, and a sense of calm challenge in the game.
- Background color: A very light, almost white, subtle blue (#F4F4FD), providing a clean and focused canvas for the trivia cards in a light scheme.
- Accent color: A vibrant, clear blue (#33A1F2) to draw attention to interactive elements and highlight important information.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for its modern, clear, and highly legible characteristics, suitable for conveying clues and answers effectively.
- Card-based layout: Emphasizes a clean, minimalist card design for each trivia question, making clues and answers easy to digest.
- Minimalist and intuitive icons: Use simple, universally understood icons for actions like 'reveal clue' and 'next card' to maintain a clean interface.
